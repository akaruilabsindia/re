Our Approach to UI/UX


Success of a product depends upon how good UI/UX has been given importance. Our UI/UX execution is a four-stage process – user-centered analysis, information design, UI design and usability analysis. While the product goes through this UI/UX workflow, understanding of the expected product gets clear to all stakeholders and requirements start making sense.

UI/UX is a critical aspect that impacts all of our product development. Our UI/UX professionals ensure that the final product looks great and delivers an amazing digital experience. They put together their understanding of human behavior, technology and design to work.

Service Integrations



Property Data providers

We integrate real estate property data from Zillow, Onboard, House Canary and Great School to help Property Managers, Real Estate Agents to prepare rich and certified property data that helps Investors in making informed decisions.

Content Management System

A successful product needs to have a good content management system that why we are focused on providing custom built intuitive and efficient CMS and integrating with CRM like Salesforce & Hubspot.

Accreditation / KYC / AML

We develop workflows for identity verification, KYC, AML, and for verifying accredited investors. Our solutions integrate with Blockscore, Trulioo, SwiftDil, InvestReady among others.



Digital Document & Signature

We integrate with Adobe Sign, HelloSign, Right Signature for digital documents such as Trust Agreements, Equity Sharing Agreements, W9 Forms with single / multiple signers options.

Blockchain

We implement one of the emerging blockchains ‘Stellar Blockchain’ not only to securely record user’s private and public data but also as a trusted payment service.

Payment Gateways

We integrate Synapsepay & Dwolla for ACH & WIRE to help Investors linking their bank accounts whereas Stripe for Credit card payments.



Authentication

In addition to common logins we provide second layer of security to user accounts by integrating some of the popular 2FA(Two Factor Authentication) services like Google Authenticator, Twilio Authy and OTP based verification.

Security

For making encrypted communications between browser and server, we use some well known ssl certificate providers like Comodo, GoDaddy, Digicert etc.
For encrypting application level data, we use cryptographic algos to ensure the privacy of user data.

Hosting

We give strength to our projects by using secure, infrastructure flexible, price per go hosting servers like Heroku and AWS
